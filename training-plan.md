# Weekly training plan

17-week Triphasic vertical jump macrocycle. Outside hitter, competitive volleyball.

## Weekly structure

| Day | Session |
|-----|---------|
| Monday | Volleyball match (speed/plyo stimulus) |
| Tuesday | Lift A — Medium, 82-87%, eccentric |
| Wednesday | Rest or mobility |
| Thursday | Volleyball (second court day) |
| Friday | Lift B — High, 90-97%, reactive singles |
| Saturday | Lift C — High volume, 75-80%, eccentric |
| Sunday | Rest |

## Sessions

### Monday — Volleyball match (speed/plyo stimulus)
- Match play or scrimmage, game reps
- Approach + swing volume, live tempo
- Serving practice, targets
- Treat the match as the week's speed and plyometric stimulus

### Tuesday — Lift A (Medium, 82-87%, eccentric)
One day removed from the match, nervous system fresh.
- Back squat, 82-87%, eccentric tempo, 4 sets
- Romanian deadlift, eccentric focus, 3 sets
- Bulgarian split squat, 3 sets per leg
- Powell raise, 3 sets per side
- Standing calf raise, 4 sets

### Wednesday — Rest or mobility
- Hip mobility flow, 10 min
- Ankle + calf mobility, wall + banded
- Thoracic openers, foam roller
- Optional light spin or walk, 20-30 min easy

### Thursday — Volleyball (second court day)
- Approach jump reps, outside hitter footwork
- Hitting lines, volume swings
- Blocking movement, lateral + closing
- Serve receive, platform consistency

### Friday — Lift B (High, 90-97%, reactive singles)
CNS recovered from Thursday.
- Back squat, 90-97%, reactive singles
- Trap bar jump, 5 sets (power substitute for power cleans)
- Weighted pull-up, 4 sets
- Push press, 4 sets
- Hanging leg raise, 3 sets

### Saturday — Lift C (High volume, 75-80%, eccentric)
- Front squat, 75-80%, eccentric tempo, higher volume
- Nordic hamstring curl, 3-4 sets
- Hip thrust, 4 sets
- Single-leg RDL, 3 sets per leg
- Powell raise, 3 sets per side
- Glute-ham raise, 3 sets

### Sunday — Rest
- Full rest: sleep, hydration, nutrition
- Optional light walk, keep it easy

## Notes
- Triphasic blocks: weeks 1-6 eccentric, 7-11 isometric, 12-17 concentric/power.
  The intensity percentages above are the baseline; adjust tempo per block.
- Three lifting days (Tue/Fri/Sat) with the high-intensity day (Fri) sitting
  before the high-volume day (Sat). Monitor fatigue across that back-to-back.
- Power cleans replaced with trap bar jumps throughout.
